About

A partner, not a vendor

Prytania Partners is an independent advisory practice based in the Netherlands. We take on a small number of engagements at a time, which lets us work closely, move quickly, and stay personally accountable for outcomes.

Our approach is simple: understand the business first, give honest advice second, and stay involved until the work is done — whether that is a strategic decision, an operational change, or a digital project brought from idea to launch.
