Investment thesis

We invest in people who can achieve VC-like returns and whose companies have a positive impact on society and the environment.

What we look for

  • Exceptional, passionate founding teams with founder-market fit
  • Companies early in the technology opportunity window
  • Innovative, scalable solutions with software-like margins
  • Sustainable and ethical business practices

How we invest

  • Active partnership: strategic support, mentoring and network beyond capital
  • Long-term perspective — impactful ventures take time to mature
  • A collaborative ecosystem among our portfolio companies

How we assess risk

  • Due diligence is about finding reasons not to invest
  • Eight risk lenses: team, market, product, financial, operational, exit, crowding-out, legal & regulatory
  • Mitigated through due diligence, diversification and active involvement

The maths

  • Typically €50–100k per investment — entry valuation is key
  • Roughly 1 in 10 angel investments becomes a home run, so each one must have home-run potential
  • Most exits take 8–12 years; we invest patiently
